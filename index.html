A full signal chain

Everything a synth needs — nothing you don't.

Built directly on the Web Audio graph, every module is a real DSP node routed and modulated in real time.

Dual-oscillator engine

Two independent oscillators with sine, triangle, sawtooth and square waves, per-oscillator detune, octave and level — the foundation of every great patch.

📈

Live oscilloscope

Waveform, spectrum and Lissajous modes with adjustable zoom and neon glow.

🎚️

ADSR envelopes

Separate amplitude and filter envelopes with a live, animated visualizer.

🌫️

Resonant filter

Low-pass, high-pass, band-pass and notch modes with envelope modulation.

Studio effects & modulation

Convolution reverb, feedback delay and stereo chorus, plus an LFO you can route to pitch, filter, amplitude or pan for vibrato, tremolo and wobble.

🎹

Playable keyboard

On-screen keys plus your computer keyboard, with octave shifting and a voice meter.
